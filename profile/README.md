# Jura Labs

> *Tools for a more honest internet.*

**Jura Labs** is a UK Community Interest Company (No. 17117467, registered 25 March 2026). We build local-first software for two domains:

- **[Jura Trace](https://github.com/Jura-Labs/jura-trace)** — desktop application for forensic verification of images, video and documents. Twelve detection signals, C2PA Content Credentials, watermarking. Runs entirely on your device. No cloud. No accounts.
- **ROOTED** — carbon-aware decision-support tool. (Public release pending.)

## Why a CIC?

A Community Interest Company has an asset lock and a community-purpose statement that cannot be quietly removed. Our community statement obliges us to keep these tools accessible to cultural institutions, journalists, educators, and frontline civil-society organisations — the people for whom commercial-only access models do not work.
